# Stanford Doerr School of Sustainability - Center for Computation

Welcome to the Stanford Doerr School of Sustainability Center for Computation! Our mission is to support and enhance research computing and data services for sustainability research at Stanford University. We manage critical resources, including the "serc" partition on the Sherlock HPC cluster and Oak data storage solutions.

## About Us

The Center for Computation focuses on providing high-performance computing and data management solutions specifically tailored for sustainability research. We strive to empower researchers with the tools they need to analyze and interpret complex data, innovate solutions, and facilitate groundbreaking discoveries.
